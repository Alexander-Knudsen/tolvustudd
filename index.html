Project 2

This project focuses on laser cutting and vinyl cutting. I designed a press-fit model in Fusion 360 using parametric modeling and tested different types of joints for a secure fit. After adjusting for kerf compensation, I cut the final version on a laser cutter and created a separate vinyl cut design using Inkscape.

Project 3

A 3D printing & scanning project where I turned the Botanic logo (from my AI chatbot service) into a layered 3D printed sculpture. I designed the model in Fusion 360, sliced it with PrusaSlicer, and printed it on a Prusa i3 MKS. For the scanning part, I used Polycam to scan a Collab energy drink.
